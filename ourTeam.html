Steve Freeman, MD

Psychologist


Steve Freeman, MD, is a board certified psychologist, expertised in psychology and psychotherapy, who is the former president of the Phama & Biotech Sales, London, England and a supervisor and a senior lecturer in the department of Biotechnology of the faculty of medicine, Oxford University.
He is passionately involved in the practise and teaching of psychotherapy and is an active supervisor currently working in the Massachusets General Hospital. Dr. Freeman is well known for his ability to cure even the serious patients with mental illnesses in his last 10 years of service in the Northampshire general hospital.

Emily Swift, phD

Psychiatrist


Emily Swift, phD, is an award-winning, board certified Psychiatrist who is expertised in Adult psychiatry and Patient Advocacy.
Dr. Swift is a staunch patient advocate and she was awarded for the Best Medical Service Advisor in the year 2015. the award was given as a result of her marvolous performance in the 60th medical advisor summit which was held in the year 2014.
Dr. Swift was also named the Medical Director of the Addiction Recovery Centre at Pensylvannia General Hospital .she is a senior lecturer in the department of psychiatry, faculty of medicine and allied sciences in the University of Massachusets.

Leesa Parker, MD

Neurologist


Leesa Parker, MD is a board certified Neurologist, who is expertised in Headache and Migraine, Stroke and Neurology.
she became a clinical instructor in the Brigham and women Hospital, Harvard University, after completing a fellowship in stroke, followef by a secondary fellowship in headache and facial pain. She contributed towards the award winning book of her, "Migraine and your health" which has won the award of best medical book in the year 2019.
she is currently engaged in an experiment based on the Neurological changes happening due to the adwherse migraine conditions of human, while working as a proffessor in the department of Neuropsychology, Faculty of Medicine in Oxford University.

Henry Samuel, MD

Child & Adolescent Psychatrist


Henry Samuel, MD is a board certified child and Adolescent Psychatrist who is expertised in psychiatry and Juveline Justice Reform, and worked most of his career for the betterment of the childrean and adolescents in the middle-East countries in the last decade.
he is a well experienced person because of the communication and the live experiences he gained from working and living in the countries which are well-known to as medically underserved communities.
after his exceptional service, he came back to England in order to do a research for his book "The child Matters.." which was published in 2013 and won the award for the best research book in the same year. Dr, Samuel now works as a professor in the department of Psychatry, University of Pensylvennia.
